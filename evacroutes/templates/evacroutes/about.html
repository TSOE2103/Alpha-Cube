User Guide

Step 1 - Download the Africa Talking App from playstore or launch the simulator

Step 2 - Login with your Mobile Number and then choose USSD

Step 3 - Enter *384*3833#

Step 4 - It has 2 Parts

Part 1

Press 1 to ask for support

It will be recorded in our database and the victim's location will be shown on our map , this location is chosen randomly .

Part 2

Press 2 to get updates

Using the updates form, the response team will be posting updates on our website , ensuring that the user gets the most recent updates through USSD.

ABOUT US

Alpha-Cube

We at Alpha Cube belive that enabling effecient communication between affected regions and relief camps will solve a major problem during natural disasters .

During any disaster the communication between the relief forces and victims becomes a major problem due to low network connectivity. Also many lives are lost due to lack of proper evacuation and placement of the relief camp. Our idea is to solve this problem by creating a USSD based messaging service which aims to bring faster connectivity between victim and relief forces. We want to focus on usability in low connection situations. We are working on giving the best location for the Emergency response team and computing the evacuation paths effectively during various natural tragedies.

Technologies Used

1) Africa Talking API - To simulate USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) application


2) Bing Maps - To display the locations of stranded people .


3) Django - Python Web Framework
